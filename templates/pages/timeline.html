Our Timeline

For more than 40 years The founders of Edu-Fun had established a large chain of schools in the Middle East. These schools were run on non-profit basis and aimed to raise the standard of education in the region.

The need for quality educational toys and equipment at affordable prices in the region became an obvious market demand to them. When Edu-Fun was first established as Educational Projects Co. in 1987, it was the first and only company in the field in the Middle East. Now its products can be found in more than 25 countries. Edu-Fun continues to be a leading company which still has many goals to achieve in order to fulfill the aim which the company was built for.
